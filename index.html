Introduction of our core members

The French Touch(TFT) is a project developed by three university students of KEDGE who major in Start-up. The project aims help the other Chinese(and every foreign students in France) live better and more comfortably in France. The reason why they three came up with this idea is that they have personaly met a lot of problems when they first arrived in France, they turned to other platforms for help but none of them really proposes solutions that suit them well. That's when thay made up their mind.
